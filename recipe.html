Spaghetti Carbonara

Submitted by: Chef Ahmad

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crisp.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve hot with extra cheese.
